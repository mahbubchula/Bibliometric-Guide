What is Bibliometric Analysis?

Click to expand

Bibliometric analysis is a quantitative research method that uses statistical and mathematical techniques to analyze academic publications, citations, and research trends. It helps researchers understand the structure and dynamics of scientific fields.

Key Applications

  • Research Trend Analysis: Identify emerging topics and declining research areas
  • Impact Assessment: Evaluate the influence of publications, authors, and institutions
  • Collaboration Patterns: Map research networks and international collaborations
  • Knowledge Structure: Understand how concepts and theories are connected
